Overview

Purpose

We want to inform customers about our rafting trips, prices and safe measurements. Customers in the comfort of their home can be able reserve their spots directly through website booking

Audience

The target audience for our water rafting website consists of active and adventurous individuals of varying ages who have a strong interest in outdoor activities. These adventure enthusiasts are seeking thrilling experiences and immersive outdoor adventures, and our website aims to cater to their needs and provide them with the information and resources necessary to plan and enjoy their rafting trips. 1 Who are the target customers for the water rafting website? Our target customers are individuals aged 18-45 who are passionate about adventure and outdoor activities. They are likely to have a moderate to high income level and prioritize experiences and travel in their lifestyle. 2 What do our target customers want? Our target customers want thrilling and adrenaline-pumping experiences in the great outdoors. They seek excitement, adventure, and the chance to challenge themselves physically and mentally. They are interested in water rafting as a way to connect with nature, enjoy camaraderie with fellow adventurers, and create lasting memories. 3 How will our target customers usually access the website?" Our target customers are tech-savvy and frequently use mobile devices to access information and make travel-related decisions. Therefore, it is important that our website is mobile-friendly and responsive, ensuring a seamless browsing experience across various devices.
